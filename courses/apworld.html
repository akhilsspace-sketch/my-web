AP World History

Complete AMSCO chapter notes for Units 1–9. Master world history from 1200 CE to present with organized, searchable notes aligned with the College Board curriculum.

Units & Chapters

Unit 1
The Global Tapestry (c. 1200–1450)
Developments in Asia, Africa, Americas, and Europe before sustained contact
Unit 2
Networks of Exchange (c. 1200–1450)
Trade routes, cultural exchange, religious spread, technology transfer
Unit 3
Land-Based Empires (c. 1450–1750)
Ottoman, Safavid, Mughal, Ming, and Qing empires
Unit 4
Transoceanic Interconnections (c. 1450–1750)
European exploration, Atlantic World, columbian exchange, slave trade
Unit 5
Revolutions (c. 1750–1900)
Political, social, and intellectual revolutions; nationalism and liberalism
Unit 6
Consequences of Industrialization (c. 1750–1900)
Industrial Revolution, economic systems, imperialism, social change
Unit 7
Global Conflict (c. 1900–present)
World Wars, ideological conflicts, weapons development, geopolitics
Unit 8
Cold War and Decolonization (c. 1900–present)
Superpower tension, decolonization movements, non-aligned nations, proxy wars
Unit 9
Globalization (c. 1900–present)
International organizations, technology, economic integration, cultural flows
